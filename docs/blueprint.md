# **App Name**: Rumonge Cultural Trails

## Core Features:

- Wonders & Attractions Directory: An interactive map and catalog of Rumonge's natural wonders and points of interest, complete with images and descriptions.
- Cultural Heritage Spotlight: Showcase sections dedicated to Rumonge's traditions, history, and local arts, including high-quality visuals and engaging text.
- AI-Powered Itinerary Planner: A tool that allows tourists to generate personalized itineraries based on their interests (e.g., nature, history, relaxation, adventure), length of stay, and preferred pace.
- Local Events & Festivals Calendar: A dynamic calendar highlighting upcoming cultural events, festivals, and local happenings in Rumonge Commune.
- Visitor Information Hub: A comprehensive guide for tourists including accommodation options, transportation, local cuisine, and essential travel tips.
- Dynamic Image & Video Gallery: A media gallery featuring high-resolution photos and videos capturing the beauty and vibrancy of Rumonge.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme, reflecting the vibrant natural beauty and inviting spirit of the region. Primary color: a rich, earthy gold (#B8630A), evoking warm sunlight and traditional crafts. Background color: a desaturated, warm off-white (#F7F1ED), providing a clean canvas. Accent color: a muted, warm brick red (#CC4D4D), to highlight important information and call-to-actions.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif), chosen for its elegant and sophisticated feel, ideal for conveying the unique allure of cultural heritage and wonders. Body text font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), offering excellent readability and a modern yet approachable aesthetic for all descriptive content and visitor information.
- Utilize icons that are modern yet culturally resonant. Prefer line-art or subtly illustrated icons that depict elements of nature, traditional symbols, local crafts, and common tourist activities.
- Employ a responsive, image-rich layout that prioritizes visual storytelling. Implement a clear card-based design for showcasing attractions and cultural content, allowing for easy scanning and discovery. Ensure ample white space for a clean, premium feel.
- Subtle, smooth animations for content loading, image transitions, and interactive map elements to enhance user engagement without distracting from the content. Gentle parallax scrolling on hero sections to add depth to visual showcases.
